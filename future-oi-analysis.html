Future OI Analysis Tool

This indicator appears to be a Futures Open Interest (OI) Analysis Tool designed to help traders understand market sentiment (long/short bias), cost of carry dynamics, and rollover behaviour. Below is a comprehensive description along with the formulas used:

🔍 What It Analyzes:

  • Price Change
  • Open Interest (OI) Data
  • Cost of Carry (COC)
  • Rollover Percentage

📊 Column Breakdown & Formulas:

  • Date: Trading date
  • Close: Spot close price
  • OI Cur: Open Interest of current month
  • OI Next: Open Interest of next month
  • COI: OI Cur + OI Next
  • COI Chg: ((Current COI - Previous COI) / Previous COI) × 100
  • Price Cng: ((Current Close - Previous Close) / Previous Close) × 100
  • OI BuildUP:
    • Long Buildup: Price ↑ & OI ↑
    • Short Buildup: Price ↓ & OI ↑
    • Short Covering: Price ↑ & OI ↓
    • Long Unwinding: Price ↓ & OI ↓

⚙️ Features:

  • Toggle visibility of table columns
  • Drop-down menu to select number of rows (table size)
  • Date-range filter to view required number of days
